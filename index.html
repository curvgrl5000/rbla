Rails Bridge LA

Community Driven Workshops

RailsBridge is dedicated to increasing diversity by helping women learn how to code.

We provide free workshops on Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Javascript and other key frameworks in the Los Angeles area.

All levels of experience welcome; from having never coded to years of experience!
